FAQs

  • Why is It Necessary to Keep Eye Siesta Running in the Background?

    Due to system limitations, only with the app running in the background can we accurately track your mobile usage. Eye Siesta is incredibly energy-efficient, consuming only 1% to 3% of your battery daily.

  • How to Maintain Running in the Background?

    Normally, the app will continue to operate in the background without any additional action required. However, events like system updates or phone reboots will interrupt this background operation; simply relaunch the app to resume.

  • What is Strict Mode?

    When Strict Mode is activated, mini and long breaks become mandatory and cannot be skipped. Additionally, Eye Siesta will automatically restrict the use of all apps, helping you focus solely on resting your eyes and mind.

  • How to Set Apps for Use in Strict Mode?

    Eye Siesta recommends avoiding a whitelist in Strict Mode, as few things are so urgent they can't wait 20 seconds.
